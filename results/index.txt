SOTHE Supplementary — Results index
====================================

validation_run.json
    Machine-readable structured output of make_validation_figure.py.
    Schema:
      run_date_utc, elapsed_seconds,
      gsl_test  = { params, Delta_S_tot_nats, eta_GSL_final,
                    fraction_local_decreases, photon_number_drift,
                    paper_range_*, passes }
      phase_matching_test = { delta3, k_RR, product_k_RR_times_delta3,
                              c_RR_fit, product_mean, product_std,
                              relative_spread_percent,
                              paper_c_RR, paper_product_mean, passes }

validation_run.txt
    Human-readable version of validation_run.json. The "PASS: True"
    lines at the bottom of each section are the certification.

entropy_trajectory.csv
    Per-step record of the nominal GSL run.
    Columns: xi, S_hor, S_rad, S_tot, eta_GSL, P_norm.
    200 rows, xi from 0 to 12.
    Photon-number drift visible in the P_norm column: < 1e-3 throughout.

phase_matching_scan.csv
    Cherenkov scan data.
    Columns: delta3, k_RR, k_RR_times_delta3.
    5 rows for delta_3 in [0.06, 0.10].
    The mean of the third column is the headline number ~1.02.

summary_run1.txt
    Production-run summary: reformulated entropy across mask schemes
    (truncated GNLSE).  Demonstrates that S_hor(0) = 2.405,
    S_tot(end) = 4.336 -> Delta S_tot = +1.93 nats, eta_GSL = 0.54
    across all three mask schemes (variance < 10^-3 between schemes).
    Addresses Referee 2 Points 1-3.

summary_run2.txt
    Production-run summary: truncated vs full GNLSE (Raman + beta_4).
    Documents the loss of optical-only GSL when Raman is active and
    its restoration once the phonon-bath term is included in the
    Clausius accounting. Addresses Referee 1 on model completeness.
